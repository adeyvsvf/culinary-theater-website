Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate most dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and various allergies. Please inform us when making your reservation so our chef can prepare accordingly.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 72 hours notice. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Can you host private events?

Yes! We offer private dining experiences for groups of 12-50 people. Our events team can create a customized menu and experience for your special occasion. Contact us directly to discuss your needs.
