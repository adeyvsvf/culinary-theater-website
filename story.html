Our Story

Where culinary artistry meets theatrical presentation, creating unforgettable dining experiences

Chef Marcus Delacroix

Executive Chef & Founder

With over two decades of culinary excellence, Chef Marcus Delacroix brings his revolutionary vision of "theatrical dining" to every plate. Trained in the finest kitchens of Paris and New York, Marcus believes that dining should engage all the senses.

"Cooking is not just about flavor," says Marcus. "It's about creating moments of wonder, stories that unfold with each bite, and memories that linger long after the meal ends."

His innovative approach combines classical French techniques with modern molecular gastronomy, creating dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.

The Theater Experience

Culinary Theater isn't just a restaurant—it's a performance venue where our open kitchen serves as the stage. Every meal is a carefully choreographed show, with our chefs as the performers and each dish as an act in an unfolding culinary drama.

Our philosophy centers on the belief that great food should tell a story. From the moment you enter our dining room, you become part of an immersive experience that engages sight, sound, smell, taste, and touch.

We source only the finest seasonal ingredients, working directly with local farmers and artisans to ensure that every component on your plate represents the peak of flavor and quality. Our menu changes with the seasons, allowing us to showcase the best that each time of year has to offer.
